FORMAT OF QORENDER SHADER CACHE FILE
VERSION 1.0
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
Overall Structure                                                                                                                     |
--------------------|---------------------------|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
Content             | Hex / String              | Length (bytes)    | Description                                                     |
--------------------|---------------------------|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
Global Header       | "QORGLSLES3BINS"          | 14                | Magic number                                                    |
                    |---------------------------|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
                    | AA II PP BB               | 4                 | QORender Version (Major, Minor, Patch, Build) since last update |
                    |---------------------------|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
                    | EE                        | 1                 | Endianness (0: Little Endian, 1: Big Endian)                    |
                    |---------------------------|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
                    | VV                        | 1                 | Protocol Version (Now it is 0x1)                                |
                    |---------------------------|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
                    | TT                        | 1                 | Backend Type (see enum QO_RenderingBackend)                     |
                    |---------------------------|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
                    | LL                        | 1                 | Length of GPU Name                                              |
                    |---------------------------|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
                    |                           | Variable (LL)     | GPU Name (Not end with null)                                    |
                    |---------------------------|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
                    | HH HH HH HH HH HH HH HH   | 8                 | Hash of GPU Name (often XXH3)                                   |
                    |---------------------------|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
                    | NN NN                     | 2                 | Number of Shaders                                               |
--------------------|---------------------------|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
Shader Chunks       | See following description for more                                                                              |
--------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
Shader Chunk
--------------------|--------------------------|--------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
Chunk Header        | "SHADER"                 | 6                  | Magic number                                                                              |
--------------------|--------------------------|--------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
Specific Shader     | (String, uppercase)      | 4                  | Magic number, identifies shader type, equal to the extension of the shader source file    |
                    |--------------------------|--------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
                    | LL LL LL LL              | 4                  | Length of Fragment Shader                                                                 |
                    |--------------------------|--------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
                    |                          | Variable (LL)      | Binary Shader                                                                             |
--------------------|--------------------------|--------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
...... (more shaders)                                                                                                                                           |
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|

Appendix
Extension of GLSL source file specified by Khronos Group:
-------|--------------------------------|
.vert  | Vertex Shader                  |
.frag  | Fragment Shader                |
.geom  | Geometry Shader                |
.tesc  | Tessellation Control Shader    |
.tese  | Tessellation Evaluation Shader |
.comp  | Compute Shader                 |
.glsl  | Generic Shader                 |
-------|--------------------------------|
